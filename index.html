Jenny Park

My oldest friend (in terms of duration of knowing each other). We met at church when we were just nine years old and have seen each other grow up into the women we are today. She's a sister to me and I've loved just growing UP with her. We've been there through it all - through all the birthdays, graduations, holidays, and family events. I'm so blessed to call her my best friend and I'm so glad this talented lady will not only be a bridesmaid but will be blessing us all with her beautiful singing on the day of. Love you to pieces! Thank you for standing with me on our day <3

Joyce Lee

I'm not really sure how we first met anymore, but in my memory, I had the opportunity to get close to Joyce through serving in praise team with her for many years. She showed me what it means to care for others and serve unselfishly. Even though I don't think I'll ever address her as so, she's been an older sister to me all these years and I'm so grateful for our long chats, even the ones we'd have at 8AM in the dingy donut shop. Thank you in advance for holding down the fort pre-wedding & standing with me on our day <3

Diane Kang

Kang & Yang!! After more than 10 years of friendship, I am so honored and blessed to have this wonderful lady as one of my bridesmaids. I spent a good deal of my high school after-school hours at her house, eating abnormal amounts of kimchi fried rice & chatting incessantly about anything and everything... but mostly strawberry ice cream and shiny purple wigs. She loved me even in my swishy yellow board shorts days and I'm forever grateful. Truly one of the sweetest and bubbliest people I have in my life. Thank you for standing with me on our day <3

Jennifer Hong

I've had the privilege of calling Jennifer my roommate/housemate for the past 4ish years and I couldn't have asked for a better friend to live with. She lets me talk 80% of the time we're together (which works out so well for the both of us) and she's been my rock for all the wild ups and downs in my life as we both approached adulthood. I love her for her sincerity, unbelievable introversion, and unexpected kick-ass attitude about pretty much everything in life. We're still months away from the wedding but seriously, you're holding down the fort. Thank you for standing with me on our day <3

Elisa Kim

We met 11 years ago, when I convinced this crazy girl to get her ears pierced because I claimed it was one of the best decisions of my life. I hardly knew her when we had this important conversation in our middle school locker room. Now, she's one of my closest and best friends and I'm so happy she's one of my bridesmaids. She's my most thoughtful & eccentric friend and even though we drive each other nuts every single time we talk, she's got a large piece of my heart. Thank you for standing with me on our day <3
